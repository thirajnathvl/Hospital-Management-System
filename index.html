Immediate First-Aid Guides

Cardiac Arrest (CPR)

Check responsiveness. Call ER. Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 compressions per min).

Severe Bleeding

Apply firm, direct pressure to the wound using clean cloth or bandage. Elevate above the heart if possible.

Burns Treatment

Cool the burn with running cool tap water for 10-20 minutes. Do NOT apply ice or butter. Cover loosely with sterile wrap.

Pediatric Choking

Give up to 5 back blows between shoulder blades, then 5 quick chest thrusts. Repeat until blockage clears.
